Bat mask cosplay

I like go to MCM London Comic Con as often as I can afford, and I like to cosplay every time. In 2014 Batgirl from DC comics got a new costume design that I really liked the look of, and thatâ€™s what I wanted to cosplay that year at May comic con.

Method: Sculpted onto a polyester head that I modified slightly to match my facial shape, then took a plaster cast mould straight over the sculpt, after the plaster was dry thoroughly cleaned it up with a toothbrush then spread some talcum powder around the inside, then mixed a thick latex mix with black acrylic paint and flour to give it bulk.

If I was to do this again I would take a cast of my head to get the correct shape as it was a little tight across the cheekbones and as I had to wear a wig, I'd also carefully smooth out the cast maybe even do a fibreglass take and then smooth that out to then create a cast off of that to get a smoother finish on the finished latex cowl.
