MAD MAX Furioa's Prosthetic Arm Replica

Here I have replicated Furiosa’s prosthetic arm from the film Mad Max: Fury Road (2015).

I decided to make this piece as the film inspired me a great deal, especially the fact that practical effects and physical props were such a big part of the movie. I chose Furiosa’s arm, as it is not only an interesting design, but it fits into the film’s aesthetic, as it makes sense that in a wasteland a prosthetic would be made out of old pieces of tools and mechanics.

Method: I made the piece using a variety of different materials including: styrene, MDF, aluminium sheet, kitchen cabinet struts, worbla, faux leather, milliput, an exhaust pipe clip and fast cast.
